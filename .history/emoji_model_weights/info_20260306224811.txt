This folder contains all of the model weights for the conversions of 28 long vectors (taken from different places) into
values for emoji, allowing emoji prediction.

Directories with 'ai' in their name mean the input vectors were taken from the emotion AI.
Directories with 'db' in their name mean the input vectors were taken from the original go_emotions_dataset.csv.
Directories with 'BCE' mean they were trained with Binary Cross-Entropy loss.
